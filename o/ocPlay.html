Play oc

Find the 1 red tile before running out of clicks!

How to play:
  • You have 5 clicks in total.
  • Find 1 red tile (never at the center).
  • Hints are given by numbers/colors:
    • 4 (2 Orange tiles): Always next to the red tile.
    • 3 (3 Yellow tiles): Always diagonal to the red tile.
    • 2 (4 Green tiles): In the same row or column as red.
    • 1 (Teal tiles): Same row, column, or diagonal as red.
    • 0 (Blue tiles): NEVER in the same row, column, or diagonal.
