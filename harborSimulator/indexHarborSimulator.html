📖 How to Play
  • Sign contracts with cargo companies to attract ships. No contracts = only small fishing vessels!
  • Each company has a trust level (★). Low trust = cheaper but risky. High trust = reliable but pricier.
  • When ships approach, accept or decline them. Use Accept All / Decline All for bulk control.
  • Dock accepted ships and assign workers. Use Smart Assign to avoid wasting labor.
  • Press "Next Month ⏭" to advance. Workers unload, revenue is collected, costs are paid.
  • Build worker infrastructure: progress from basic housing to a full Company Town.
  • Choose your labor path: support unions for stability or bust them for profit.
  • Hire private security to protect against mafia and improve safety.
  • Watch for world events that shift commodity markets — adapt your contracts!
  • Beware of random events — storms, pirates, dock strikes, the mafia, and worse!
  • The government changes every 4 years. Lawful = order. Corrupt = chaos and opportunity.
