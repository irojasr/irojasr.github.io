⚓ Harbor Simulator

Sign contracts. Dock ships. Build your empire.

📖 How to Play
  • Sign contracts with cargo companies to attract ships. No contracts = only small fishing vessels!
  • Each company has a trust level (★). Low trust = cheaper but risky (cargo may arrive spoiled). High trust = reliable but pricier.
  • When ships approach, accept or decline them. Declining costs rep; accepting and failing to dock is much worse.
  • Dock accepted ships and assign workers. Each ship type has a max effective crew — extra workers beyond that won't help.
  • Press "Next Month ⏭" to advance. Workers unload, revenue is collected, wages and fees are paid.
  • Build worker infrastructure (housing, markets, benefits) to improve efficiency and morale.
  • Watch your contracts' satisfaction: turn away too many ships and companies will terminate!
  • Beware of random events — storms, pirates, dock strikes, and the mafia!
  • The government changes every 4 years. Lawful governments crack down on crime; corrupt ones enable it.
